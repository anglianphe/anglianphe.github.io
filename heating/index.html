Boilers

Gas - All our engineers are fully Gas Safe registered and we also hold the rare LPG ticket for mobile homes and where no natural gas is available.

Oil - We are an OFTEC oil registered business, for all oil boiler and oil tank repair and replacements.

From new flues to new tanks and bases we do everything to get your heating back up and running with the minimum of fuss. All installed to the current strict regulations be it gas, LPG, oil or electrical.

We carry out all repairs and servicing to nearly all types and makes of boilers by all manufacturers, be it Baxi, Potterton, Ideal,Grant, Worcester Bosch, Main, Alpha, Vokera, Vaillant... to name but a few.

Combination boilers no problem.

We repair and replace with full 10 and 12 year warranty on some boilers. Please ask for our special all inclusive prices which all include full power flush to radiator system, all labour and materials, and VAT.

Heating hot water via a boiler or emersion can both give issues either from broken zone valves or 2-port and 3-port valves giving no hot water or hot radiators when hot water is demanded.

Broken thermostats and faulty circulation pumps can also cause issues.

It is recommended to get unvented and gravity fed hot water cylinders serviced as well as regular boiler servicing.

We can diagnose all faults on faulty central heating.
